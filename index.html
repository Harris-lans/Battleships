RULES FOR THE CLASSIC MODE:
Players are tasked to destroy all the ships hidden before the Missiles run out.

RULES FOR THE 2PLAYER:
This is a turn-based mode where the players goal is to destroy the other players fleet. The 'Switch Map' Button can be used by the players to view their maps with their ship's position. The announcer pops up after every turn asking the players to switch.
